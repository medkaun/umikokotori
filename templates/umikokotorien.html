Sea child with a small bird heart.
In Eternal play on the shore.

Audio visual album “In the nowhere without the no”

I am Animal I meet in boredom.
It is the desire of happiness left in its pure state.
The process of life and death is so fantastic, I live as if never going to die, and i am already dying.

While man always has the world before him – always only stands “facing opposite” and never enters “pure space” of the outside – the animal instead moves in the open “in a nowhere without the no”
This album is about desire to expand inner nature, who is dancing as life it self.
About animality of the inside, the instinct, the feel of what comes from within.
About the company of two wolfs who i teach to howl harmonically on my own funeral.
About holding the mirror and trying to see who are you not, trying to grasp, how much of the man in the monkey there are.
