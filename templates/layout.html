IN THE

Audio visual album

I am running like animal with a memory.
But no memories.
In the nowhere without the no.

While man always has the world before him - always only stands
"facing opposite" & never enters "pure space" of the outside - the
animal instead moves in the open "in a nowhere without the no"

This album is about desire to expand inner nature, who is dancing
as life itself. About animality of the inside, the instinct, the feel of
what comes from within. About the company of two wolfs who i
teach to howl harmonically on my own funeral. About holding the
mirror & trying to see who are you not, trying to grasp, how much
of the man in the monkey there are. But you excluded your self
from the nature to recognize your self, and became a "Resembling
man" who must recognize him self in a non man in order to be a
human. Homo as the animal it is only if it recognizes that it is not.
Homo is constitutively nonhuman, he can receive all natures and
all faces

I am Animal I meet in boredom.
It is the desire of happiness left in its pure state. The process of life
& death is so fantastic, that I live like we never going to die, & i am
already. How one should play in the now is a question for me, not
for the sea... Fearless is needed, not the brave, not the coward.
Fearless is needed to see that one does not know. That he is too
running in the nowhere... is it without the no?
